Delete Your Account

We're sorry to see you go. Here's how to delete your account and data.

Option 1 — Delete from within the App

  • Open the RAE app and sign in.
  • Go to Profile → Settings.
  • Tap Delete Account.
  • Confirm deletion. Your account and all data will be permanently deleted.

Option 2 — Request via Email

If you cannot access the app, email us and we will delete your account within 7 business days.

Include your registered phone number in the email.

Email Us to Delete Account

What gets deleted

  • Permanently deleted: Your account, phone number, all skin scan photos, analysis results, routines, and diary entries.
  • Retained temporarily: Transaction records may be kept for up to 90 days as required by law.
  • Subscription: Deleting your account does not automatically cancel your subscription. Cancel it separately in the Google Play Store or App Store before deleting.
Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All your skin diary progress will be lost.

Delete only your data (without deleting your account)

You can delete individual scan photos and diary entries from within the app without deleting your account. Go to Diary → tap any scan → Delete.

To delete all your data but keep your account, email us at operations@sentrainnovations.in.
